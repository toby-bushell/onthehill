Real Pizza

After time in Italy learning about the respect for the dough and process, I felt compelled to bring this authentic experience back to the UK.

With imported italian flour we hand knead and cold ferment our dough for 48 hours for complex flavours to develop. Combined with the finest fresh ingredients and classic modern pairings, we bring you taste of how pizza should be.
